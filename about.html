Our Story

In the summer of 2023, I had the opportunity to shadow at a hospital in Bangalore, where I witnessed firsthand the harsh realities of cancer treatment. Patients undergoing chemotherapy and radiation suffered from severe side effects, and it became evident that the treatments themselves were contributing significantly to their discomfort. During my time there, I met a doctor who specialized in vitamin C therapy, aiming to strengthen patients' immune systems by injecting high doses of vitamin C. This approach showed promising results, with fewer side effects compared to the conventional treatments I had observed earlier. This experience ignited my passion for exploring more effective and less harmful methods of cancer treatment, particularly through the use of targeted therapies that deliver inhibitors directly to cancer cells.

Our Team

Aditi K

Aditi Kuthanoor is the CEO and co-founder of International Children’s Wellness (ICW), dedicated to raising awareness about childhood cancer and promoting early screening. With a deep commitment to cancer research, Aditi has led initiatives that have significantly impacted communities and advanced research efforts. Beyond leading ICW, Aditi serves on the Youth Advisory Board for the State of Washington with NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness), where she advocates for mental awareness and support. She is also a youth international speaker and has advocated for mental health for various international summits. She won first prize at the Washington State Science and Engineering Fair (WSSEF) for cancer research. Additionally, Aditi is recognized as a Global Health Youth Leader for Harvard Vision. With a passion for making a difference, Aditi continues to drive ICW towards supporting those affected by childhood cancer.
